Kangaroo

On land kangaroos only ever move their hind legs together, however in water they kick each leg independently to swim.

  • Scientific Name: Macropodidae
  • Average Length: 4.25 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Australian woodlands

Kangaroos are large marsupials that are found only in Australia. They are identified by their muscular tails, strong back legs, large feet, short fur and long, pointed ears.
